Welcome to Spin & Sound Skating

Seattle's premier roller rink by day, silent disco club by nightW Where nostalgia and nightlife collide in the heart of the U-District. Whether you're lacing up skates for a Saturday afternoon session or dancing under the lights at our 21+ silent disco, we're here to bring the energy.

From families and first-timers to UW students and night owls, everyone finds their rhythm at Spin & Sound. Our vibrant space welcomes skaters of all ages and abilities during the day, then transforms into Seattle's most innovative nightlife experience after dark.

Two Experiences Under One Roof

At Spin & Sound, we believe movement and music bring people together. That's why we've created a dual-purpose venue that serves our diverse University District community in multiple ways. Come for the nostalgic roller skating, stay for the cutting-edge silent disco experience.

Roller Skating Sessions

Our roller rink welcomes skaters of all ages and abilities. Glide across our smooth floor during open skate sessions, join us for themed nights, or book a private party. With a nostalgic vibe and modern touches, skating at Spin & Sound feels like a throwback with all the right updates. All ages welcome until 9pm.

Silent Disco Club Nights

When the clock strikes 10pm on Friday and Saturday nights, the rink transforms into Seattle's most innovative nightlife experience. Dance to four different DJ channels through wireless headphones in an intimate space where you control the vibe. Our silent disco is exclusively for guests 21 and older, with full bar service available throughout the night.

How Silent Disco Works

Wireless Headphones
Upon entry, you'll receive wireless headphones that let you switch between four live DJ channels.
Four Music Channels
Choose from hip-hop, electronic dance music, throwback hits, or indie alternative. All playing simultaneously on different channels.
Control Your Experience
Adjust your volume, switch channels anytime, or take off your headphones to chat with friends at the bar.
Full Bar Service
Our snack bar transforms into a full-service bar with craft cocktails, local beers, and wine selections.
