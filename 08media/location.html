Getting Here

By Public Transportation

Multiple Metro bus routes serve the University District with stops within walking distance of our location. The U-District Light Rail Station is approximately a 10-minute walk from Spin & Sound Skating.

Parking

Street parking is available on University Way NE and surrounding side streets. Several paid parking garages are located within two blocks of our venue. Please note that street parking has time limits and restrictions - check posted signs carefully.

By Bike

Bike racks are available directly in front of our building. The Burke-Gilman Trail is nearby for cyclists coming from north or south Seattle.

Accessibility

Spin & Sound Skating is wheelchair accessible with an accessible entrance, restrooms, and seating areas. Please contact us in advance if you have specific accessibility needs so we can ensure the best possible experience for your visit.
