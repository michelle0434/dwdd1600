Roller Skating at Spin & Sound

Welcome to Seattle's most nostalgic yet modern roller skating experience. Whether you're a seasoned skater or it's your first time lacing up, our rink offers a welcoming environment for everyone. We believe roller skating is for all ages and abilities, and we're here to help you find your groove on eight wheels.

Our University District location features a smooth skating surface, vibrant atmosphere, and a carefully curated playlist that keeps the energy high throughout every session. From family skate days to themed nights, there's always something exciting happening at Spin & Sound.

First Time Skating?

Never been on roller skates before? No problem! Our friendly staff is here to help you get started. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to get fitted for skates and receive a quick orientation. Here's what to expect during your first visit.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothing that allows easy movement
  • Socks (required for skate rentals)
  • Water bottle to stay hydrated
  • Small bag for personal items (lockers available)

Safety Tips for Beginners

  • Start slow and stay near the wall until you find your balance
  • Bend your knees slightly and keep your weight centered
  • Look forward, not down at your feet
  • Use the handrail when needed, there's no shame in learning
  • Take breaks when you need them

Rink Rules & Etiquette

To ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience, we ask all skaters to follow these basic guidelines during their visit.

Safety Rules

  • Always skate in the same direction as other skaters
  • No roughhousing, racing, or skating backwards during busy sessions
  • Keep food and drinks off the skating floor
  • Children under 8 must be accompanied by an adult on the rink
  • Report any spills or hazards to staff immediately
